我觉得中国的一些地方有些怪怪的，在中国，歧视过同性恋群体的明星艺人竟然还发展的越来越好了。比如某东、某现、某柏然、某战，等等等等。你可能会说我在拉仇恨，好吧，其实这只是一种生气与愤怒、抱怨一种社会的不公，但是没有很多仇恨在这里。试问一下，歧视同性恋的明星艺人难道不算是劣迹艺人吗？我觉得他们和劣迹艺人没有什么不同。

歧视与仇恨在世界各地都存在，但是爱与友善也同样存在。在日本和韩国，既有男明星为LGBT发声，也不缺少又女明星为我们发声，中国的一些明星艺人真的应该像邻国的同辈们学习学习。而在中国也是有好人的，比如胡军，他出演过《蓝宇》，其实出演过同性题材电影这样一个身份背景不是那么的重要，重要的是他没有对同性恋等少数群体有恶意，相反的是充满了友善，从一些媒体的采访中，我们看到了他对少数群体的理解，可能还有着一份默默的支持。看到这些，我们很欣慰，心里真的暖暖的。

昨天晚上看了电影频道的《尼古拉斯·尼克贝》，这是一部改编自英国作家狄更斯同名小说的电影。我当然是觉得非常好看了，可能也会调侃到，英国的电影公司是不是没有什么资金呢，总是和美国合拍电影，还是说英国和美国真的关系太紧密了？英国、伦敦，真的是很多人都向往的一个地方，那里有《雾都孤儿》、《故园风雨后[旧地重游]》等传奇的故事，还有牛津、剑桥和《莫瑞斯》。文艺界不应该掺杂政治，但是政治又确确实实的会影响到文学与艺术、影响社会。

英国不再是以前那个英国，世界不再是以前那个世界，中国也不再是以前那个受尽列强霸凌的中国。需要明确的指出，英国、美国等一些西方国家不应该插手干涉中国的内政[包括但不限于中国香港地区、中国台湾地区、中国的新疆/西藏地区]。中国不会干涉英国的苏格兰是否要举行独立公投，更不会干涉美国大选，所以也请英国等国家做好自己的事情，当局者管理好自己的国家，不要干涉别国内政，不要妖魔化中国。

欢迎多来中国看一看、走一走、玩一玩，增进了解，增添一份善意与友好。西方国家的公民应该真实的、真正的了解中国，了解后你可能就会爱上这里，不要太敌对，不要太偏见。

I think some places in China are a little weird. In China, celebrities who have discriminated against gay groups are getting better and better. Such as certain Dong, certain Xian, certain Bairan, certain war, etc. You might say that I am pulling hatred, well, in fact, this is just a kind of anger and anger, complaining about a kind of social injustice, but there is not much hatred here. May I ask, isn’t a star artist who discriminates against homosexuality a bad artist? I think they are no different from bad artists.

Discrimination and hatred exist all over the world, but love and kindness also exist. In Japan and South Korea, there are male celebrities who speak for LGBT, and there is no shortage of female celebrities who speak for us. Some celebrities in China should really learn from their peers in neighboring countries. There are also good people in China. For example, Hu Jun, who has starred in “Lan Yu”, in fact, has appeared in same-sex movies, such an identity background is not so important. The important thing is that he is not malicious towards homosexuals and other minorities. On the contrary, It is full of friendliness. From some media interviews, we have seen his understanding of minority groups and may have a silent support. Seeing these, we are very pleased, and our hearts are really warm.

I watched “Nicholas Nick Bay” on the movie channel last night. This is a film adapted from the novel of the same name by the British writer Dickens. Of course I think it’s very good-looking, and I may also ridicule, do the British film companies have no funds and always co-produce films with the United States, or do they really have too close a relationship with the United States? Britain and London are really a place that many people yearn for. There are legendary stories such as “Orphan in the Fog”, “After the Storm in the Home Garden [Revisit the Old Place]”, as well as Oxford, Cambridge and “Morris”. The literary and art world should not be mixed with politics, but politics will indeed affect literature and art and society.

Britain is no longer the same Britain, the world is no longer the previous world, and China is no longer the previous China that was bullied by the great powers. It needs to be clearly pointed out that some Western countries such as the United Kingdom and the United States should not interfere in China’s internal affairs [including but not limited to Hong Kong, China, and China’s Xinjiang/Tibet area]. China will not interfere in whether Scotland will hold an independence referendum in the United Kingdom, let alone intervene in the U.S. election. Therefore, it also asks the United Kingdom and other countries to do their own things, and the authorities manage their own countries, not interfere in other countries’ internal affairs, and do not demonize China. .

You are welcome to come to China to see, walk, and have fun to enhance understanding and add a sense of goodwill and friendship. Citizens of Western countries should truly and truly understand China, and you may fall in love with it after you understand it. Don’t be too hostile or prejudiced.
